Artists of the American West.
















Visual artists depicting the 18th−19th century western American Frontier and American Old West, and the 20th−21st century Western United States, in various artistic media.
Artworks of this American Western genre/period/region are also referred to as "Western Art," distinct from Western art of European Art history.
